# LastDay
## Play as Robert, a man out of time, as he makes his final decision. The simple scene juxtaposes the complexities of a long marriage and secrets witheld over the years. Help Robert remember his past by finding answers to the personal security questions that gaurd his access to a document he needs to sign. Interact with Martha and Dave, his wife and best friend, in an organic fashion through typed dialogue to uncover the truth.
